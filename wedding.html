Lodging Information

If you prefer a hotel, we have set aside some rooms at the Crowne Plaza Lansing, please contact us for information. Otherwise, AirBnBs are plentiful in the area for larger parties.

Flying to Lansing

We recommend arranging your flights to arrive at the Lansing airport. The next nearest airport is the Gerald R. Ford airport in Grand Rapids Michigan, approximately 50 minutes away by car from Lansing. The next nearest airport after that is the Detroit airport, which is approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes from Lansing by car.

Getting around Lansing

Car rentals are available at the Lansing Airport. Uber/Lyft are plentiful in the area and relatively cheap. Lansing covers a large area so most travel will require a car.
